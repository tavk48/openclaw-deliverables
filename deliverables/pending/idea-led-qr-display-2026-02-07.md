## THE IDEA
Instead of table tents, create a **portable LED-backlit QR display** for musicians — think "mini Times Square" for their tip jar area.

## THE PROBLEM
- Paper table tents get ignored in dark venues
- Musicians need something that cuts through the ambiance
- LED signs are perceived as "professional" and get more engagement
- Competitors (Venmo/CashApp) just use phone screens or handwritten notes

## THE PRODUCT
**GloVibe™** — Battery-powered LED QR display box
- 6x6 inch black acrylic frame with built-in LED strip
- USB-rechargeable (lasts 6-8 hours per gig)
- Custom QR code printed on translucent insert
- "Scan to Tip + Request Songs" branding
- Adjustable brightness for any venue
- Magnetic base or clamp option

## COST STRUCTURE
- Manufacturing cost: $18-25/unit (bulk from Alibaba/custom shop)
- Retail price: $75-99 direct to musician
- **Margin:** 60-70%
- Compare to: Custom table tents ($15-30), generic LED signs ($40-60)
